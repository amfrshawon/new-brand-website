How It Works

Three simple steps between "I want to sell my car" and cash in hand.

01

Get the Offer

Enter your VIN or license plate and a few details about your car's condition. Our system generates a real cash offer in seconds — no obligation to accept.

02

Schedule Pickup

Accept your offer and pick a pickup window that works for you. We come to your driveway, office, or repair shop — free towing included, even for non-running cars.

03

Instant Payment

Hand over the keys and title, and our driver hands you a check on the spot. No waiting for a bank transfer or a check in the mail.

What You'll Need

  • Your vehicle's title (or see our team if it's missing — requirements vary by state).
  • A valid photo ID matching the name on the title.
  • The keys, if available (we can still make an offer without them).

What Condition Can My Car Be In?

Any condition. Running or not, damaged, high-mileage, or totaled — we make an offer either way. See our repair estimate vs. cash offer comparison if you're weighing whether to fix it up first.
