Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you sell.

How fast can you pick up my car?
In most cases, we can schedule a pickup within 24 hours of you accepting our offer. We work around your schedule.
Do I need the title to sell my car?
In many cases, yes. Requirements vary by state. If you're missing your title, contact us and we can help you navigate the process.
When do I get paid?
The tow driver hands you a check on the spot when they pick up the vehicle. No waiting for mail or bank transfers.
Does the car need to be running?
Absolutely not. We buy cars in any condition — running, not running, damaged, or totaled.
Is there a fee to get an offer?
No. Getting a quote is free and comes with no obligation to accept.
What areas do you serve?
We operate across Texas with 200+ pickup locations. See our Locations page for details.
